Camping

Sri Lanka Camping

Camping in Sri Lanka can be the best experience you can have while being close to nature. Amidst the scenic jungle location you have a chance to go for rainforest camping which will allow you to get deeply absorbed in the sound of the water being closer to the fall. One can couple the camping expedition with other water activities such as water rafting, bird watching and trekking to waterfalls amongst others. You can have the same concept in a jungle safari too which is an equally thrilling experience. And you can hear the sound of the animals clearly since it is located right in heart of the jungle.

01

Meemure Camping

Camping tents situated in Rural Meemure, where you can fall in love with nature completely. Only for nature lovers not for luxury seekers. Our campsite is located just next to the Heen river and we provide traditional food, Camp fire, BBQ for every guest. Also if you need transport we can arrange transport island wide.

Location: Meemure , Hunnasgiriya, Sri Lanka

02

Belihuloya Camping

The Treehouse, which is perched on an old Gliricidia Tree, can be accessed by climbing a massive boulder. Once nestled inside on a comfortable double bed with a bird’s-eye view of the Campground, we guarantee you wouldn’t want to go anywhere. The elevated, private patio of the Treehouse is ideal for a romantic dinner, under the stars!

Location: Balangoda, Belihul Oya, Sri Lanka

03

Yala National Park Camping

The sensational Yala wildlife National Park promises unparallel thrill and excellent adventurous experience with a visual treat of scenic excellence in the exclusive Yala Safari Camping. It is indeed an extra ordinary experience to rise up to a place where for miles there is no soul visible except the caretakers.

Location: Thalgasmankada, Sri Lanka

04

Kitugala Camping

Wake up with the melodious chirping of birds all around at the Rainforest Camping in the Kitugala Forest Reserve. The camping in the forest reserve of Kitugala is an experience in itself and will let you have a blend of both adventure and leisure at the same time.

Location: The camping base is approximately 2hrs 30 minutes’ drive from Colombo.

05

Knuckles Mountain Camping

Based near Matale and Kandy districts of Sri Lanka, the Knuckles mountain range is famous for its beautiful scenic expedition and the salubrious and harmonious air that provides rejuvenation to the human soul in every way possible. Getting close to the exceptional beauty of Nature, one can truly seek a peace of mind here.

Location: Central Sri Lanka close to Kandy

06

Udawalawe National Park Camping

Spreading over an area of 31,000 hectares of land, Udawalawe National Park is famed for its huge elephant population marking to a figure of 600-700 in total. So catching a glimpse of elephants herds is not very unusual in this park. This also serves as a transit home where orphaned or injured elephants are kept for treatment and after few days again released in the park.

Location: Monaragala, Uva Province, Sri Lanka

07

Vaddha Village Camping

They believe they are the descendants of them. A camping in this place can turn out to be a pretty interesting experience as one can meet the people of this clan up close and know more about their living styles and their culture

Location: Uva Province, Sri Lanka

08

Narangala camping

A hike follows camping in Narangala to the upper section of the mountain where one gets to witness the most breathtaking sceneries. If one could reach there in the early morning, they will be able to catch a glimpse of the beautiful sunrise. Kinakale is a nearby small town where people can park vehicles near an old tea factory and start hiking Narangala.

Location: Badulla District, Uva Province, Sri Lanka
